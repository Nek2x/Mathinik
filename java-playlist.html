Hypothesis Testing

Is used to make decisions or inferences about population parameters based on sample data. It begins with an assumption (called the null hypothesis) and uses evidence from the sample to determine whether to reject or not reject that assumption. It is widely used in research, business, medicine, and other fields to validate or challenge conclusions.
